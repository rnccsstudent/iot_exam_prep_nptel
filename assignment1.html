1)IoT stands for ____________.





IoT stands for Internet of Things. It refers to a system of interconnected physical devices that can collect and exchange data over the internet. These "things" can include everything from smart home appliances and wearable devices to industrial sensors and connected vehicles.

The evolution of IoT (Internet of Things) is largely due to advancements in high-power embedded systems. These systems combine processing power with sensors and connectivity, enabling devices to collect and exchange data efficiently.
